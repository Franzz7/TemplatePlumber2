02 / What We Do

"We focus on doing fewer things, properly."

01

Bathroom Installation

Full bathroom refits and design-led renovations for period and contemporary properties. We project-manage the whole job, including tiling, electrics, and decoration coordination.

02

Boiler Installation & Servicing

Worcester, Vaillant, and Viessmann installations with manufacturer-backed warranties. Annual servicing for existing systems.

03

Heating Systems

Full central heating design and installation, including underfloor heating and zoned controls for larger homes.

04

Leak Detection

Non-invasive thermal imaging and acoustic detection. We find the problem before we open up a wall.

05

Period Property Specialism

Lead pipe replacement, cast iron radiator restoration, and sensitive plumbing work for listed buildings and pre-1920 homes.

06

Emergency Callouts

Existing clients can reach us out-of-hours for emergencies. New emergency enquiries are taken case by case.

03 / How We Work

Our approach is straightforward. We arrive when we say we will. We give you a clear, fixed quote before any work begins. We work tidily and we clean up properly. We use the right materials for the job, even when nobody's looking. When something's harder than expected, we tell you — we don't surprise you with the bill.

We're a small team. We don't take on every job that comes our way. The ones we do take, we do properly.

TH
